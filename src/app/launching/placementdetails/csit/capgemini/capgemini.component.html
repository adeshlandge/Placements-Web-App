About Capgemini

Capgemini is a global leader in consulting, technology and outsourcing services. It is headquartered in Paris, France. The company has over 1,80,000 employees and has its presence in over 40 countries across the world. Capgemini has a revenue of around 11 billion Euros every year. The company has received numerous awards from well-reputed organisations like SAP, CISCO, Ethisphere Institute, and many others. It is the dream of many fresh graduates to get recruited by this global leader.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
  • Should have completed Graduation BTech/B.E./M.Tech/M.E. and MCA all branches are eligible.
  • MSc with Computer Sciences and IT are also eligible.
  • Candidate must possess more than 60% on average in Class X, XII and Graduation
  • Should have no current backlog while applying
